Email to Inbox via Bcc by admin on November 2, 2012

We’ve been working on this for awhile, and we’re happy to announce that you can now direct email to your taskmanager Inbox as a Bcc: recipient. :-) Say what? OK, in case you didn’t know, taskmanager lets you create actions by email. When you send an email to your taskmanager account, the email subject becomes the action’s name, and the email body becomes the action’s notes. You can find your Inbox address on your taskmanager account dashboard, or by selecting Getting Started from the pulldown menu on the top-right of the screen (when you’re logged in to https://app.taskmanager.com/).

While you could always send yourself an email as a normal To: or Cc: recipient, you can now include your taskmanager inbox address using the Bcc: field, which comes in handy when you’re sending an email to someone and want to send a copy to your taskmanager Inbox without your recipients knowing about your taskmanager Inbox email address. An added bonus: this also means that you can set up forwarding rules in mail apps such as Gmail to (for example) automatically forward incoming email with specific search patterns to also arrive in your taskmanager inbox. Very cool.
